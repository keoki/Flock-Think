FlockThink is a project by Keoki Seu. The sentiment analysis is written in Python and uses the scikit-learn toolkit. The training set was collected from the Twitter stream over a couple of weeks, with the presence of emoticons to indicate positive or negative sentiment.

To process the training set, usernames and urls are replaced with generic tokens. Tweets less than four words or ones that have less than 75% english words are removed. A scikit-pipeline is formed that considers bigram and trigram word fragments before being fedd to a multinominal Naive Bayes algorithm. Currently, the accuracy of the algorithm is 79%.

The website is built using Twitter Bootstrap and Flask, and D3.js is used for visualization.
